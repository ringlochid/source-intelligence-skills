# Safety Boundaries

## Allowed by default

- public web pages, public docs, public repos, public papers
- local files already in scope
- read-only source gathering and summarization
- source-watchlist reads/updates when requested

## Ask first

- account cookies or logged-in sources
- private URLs, private repos, or personal data
- scraping at scale or ToS-hostile automation
- service deployment, schedulers, monitors, or external writes
- posting, messaging, purchasing, subscribing, or changing account state

## Sensitive data

Do not store credentials, cookies, tokens, raw private URLs, or unnecessary PII in watchlists, artifacts, or summaries.

## Social sources

Treat X/Twitter/Reddit/HN/Product Hunt/community posts as leads or sentiment unless verified elsewhere.

## Financial safety

Do not present investment advice, financial advice, price targets, or buy/sell recommendations; provide sourced data/context with timestamps and caveats.
